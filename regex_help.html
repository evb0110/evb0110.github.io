NB! At present \w and \b only work in the latest builds of the Chrome browser!
In other browsers, please use \S (non-space character) instead of \w.

Anchor:

^    beginning of the line


Meta symbols:

.    any symbol including space

\b   word border (i.e. word beginning or end)

\w   any letter (not a space, not a punctuation mark)


Quantifiers (put after a symbol or a meta symbol):

*    any number of symbols including zero

+    any number of symbols, but not zero

?    0 or 1 symbol
