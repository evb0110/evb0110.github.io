(1) awwalča wayba nesəpṯil mawṯa p-ṭiflō ʕalya baḥar. (2) atar wōb ōṯ mʕarrṯa p-šenna erraʕ m-mar sarkes elʕel. (3) bess yīmuṯ mett ṭefla iẓʕur, yaʕni ʕomre yarḥa aw ešna l-ḥatti arpaʕ ḥammeš išən, massḳille tafnille elʕel, ḳabrille b-ōm mʕarrṯa. (4) yaʕni sōleḳ ḳašīša ʕimmayy w arpaʕ ḥammeš zalman m-ti maxiṣṣill lanna ṭefla. (5) sōleḳ w əmṣall aʕle, w laffille bə-ḳmōša w mišwille b-ōm mʕarrṯa w nōḥčin. (6) lā mišwin santūḳa w lā mišwin warta w lā mišwin mett b-awwalča. (7) miʕčaḳtin atar mišwill ṭiflō baləḥḏinn, liɁanno awwalča miʕčaḳtin inno hanna ṭefla ṣōlaḥ yaʕni, ex milōxa, čūṯ ġappe mett, w ṯēn mett, ōṯ minnayy yīb ḳayya ču mʕammḏin, w ōṯ minnayy yīb mʕammḏin w lā šawwiyyin awwal <ar> Ɂurbāne </ar> w lā mett. (8) atar ʕayattil xann mišwill ṭiflō baləḥḏinn, ču lōzem yaʕni yišwunn ʕemmir rappō, miʕčabrillun ṣōlḥin liɁann hann. (9) amma hōš, hōš ṯiḳnaṯ nesəpṯil mawṯa p-ṭiflō ḳallīla, lorkaʕ assḳunn l-elʕel ʕa mʕarrṯa. (10) iṯḳen lōb ameṯ mett ṭefla aspille ʕa maḳbarča l-ʕāde. (11) bess xett yaʕni ṣlōṯa l-ʕāde w aspille. (12) lā mišwin warta w lā mišwin mett. (13) amma iza ʕomre ešbaʕ ṯmōn išən, mišwille žnōzča rasmōy.
