(1) wōṯ aḥḥaḏ yabrūḓay b-maʕlūla. (2) zalle, batte yzelle ʕ yabruḓ. (3) allex ʕemmlə ʕrōba, ʕirpaṯ... ʕičmaṯ tunya aʕle, lorkaʕ iḥəm yallex. (4) mō batte yišw hanna yabruḓō? (5) čūle ġēr iḥəm sažərṯil ġawzō, isleḳ w ḳʕōle p-ḥaṣṣa. (6) hū w isleḳ ʕal-ōs sažərṯa ḳʕōle, willa ṯōle toppa. (7) atar hanna toppa uxxul yōma ṯēle, ōxel ġawzō w zelle. (8) isleḳ hanna toppa, iḳṭaf ġawza w šūne ʕl‿īḏe w čappre. (9) atar hanna toppa mattl‿īḏe ʕ nohriṣ ṣahra ḥetta ylaḳḳṭell ġawzō maʕ ḳilfō. (10) waḳčil mattl‿īḏe, yabruḓō mō mxammen? (11) yabruḓō mxammen inno ʕamḓayyefle. (12) ḳōyem ṯēle yabruḓō m-zawʕe atar amelle: “ē, xōl b-īḏax inəpṣaṭ!” (13) sōḳeṭ maʕ ġawzṯa, ṯōḳen b-arʕa.
