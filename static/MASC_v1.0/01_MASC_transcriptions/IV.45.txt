(1) ōṯ šappa, batte yixṭub eḥḏa atamōy. (2) zelle laʕl-ō, mamrille rezla, w hōr rezla. (3) ōxer mett ahtunne ʕl‿eḥḏa, ōmar meʕla atamōy. (4) xaṭba w kallel aʕla. (5) awwal yōma ōmar batte yġarrbenna, yiḥəm ʕa minžat atamōy. (6) zelle mayṯēẓ ẓabərṯe w ḏahella lawna ikkum, lawna iṣfur, lawna summuḳ. (7) hū w ʕapper leʕla, mʕaynya bā xann, minsaṭla mamrōle: “of, exma iḥmiṯ, bess ext‿anna lā iḥmiṯ!”
