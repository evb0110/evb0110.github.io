(1) ʕēḏəl barbōra maḥkem b-arpʕa xanunō awwalnō. (2) miḥčaflin bē baḥar bə-blōtaḥ w b-ġayrlə blōta. (3) mišwin iḥəl, išher hanna ʕēḏa b-iḥəl, p-xōl iḥəl. (4) šōlḳin ḥiṭṭō. uxxuš šunīṯa p-payṯa mišwa l-ʕaylṯa ḥiṭṭō w ḳaṭōyef w šiklōyəl iḥəl baḥar w ṭiflō minbaṣṭin bē. (5) mxassyin ʕa ffayy w mišwin <ar> maṣāxer </ar> w mižčamʕin xullun sawa w mintōrin, tōḳḳin w rōḳḏin w zōʕḳin: (6) barbōra ya barbōra, yā ḳattēšča muxtōra (7) ṯōle ōbuš šannis sayfa yimḥinniš, iṯḳen sayfa ṣunnōra. (8) w mḓayifillun, uxxul payṯa mḓayifillun bē pšōṯa w ṯinō w ġawzō w mlabbas w šukalāta w mappillun ḳiršō. (9) mwazzʕillun ʕa baʕḓinn w minbaṣṭin baḥar.
