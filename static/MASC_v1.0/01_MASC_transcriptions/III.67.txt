(1) orḥa nībin b-matrasṯa, ana w berčil ʕaməṯ, ušma nāyfe. (2) nifḳinnaḥ m-matrasṯa w nallīxin ʕa ṭarfis sayla. (3) ōṯ sayla, ḳayya ikšef. (4) amrilla: “uppiš čallix w əṭṭammṣill ʕaynōš?” (5) ōmar: “ē!” (6) allxaṯ ʕal-ōḥ ḥaffṯa, willa salčaṯ, ṯalla l-erraʕ. (7) ana ḥmičča — salčaṯ w eḏma iṯḳen nōfeḳ m-rayša — šamṭiṯ w zlill.
